About Me

I am a software developer with expertise in Java web development using the Spring Framework. I’ve worked on several Agile teams with a focus on shipping large enterprise web applications used by thousands of individuals.

My current position is a Mid-Level Software Developer at MAXIMUS for the NY applications team, where I designed, implemented and tested improvements to their existing application suite as well new applications. Our team's mission was to bring new creative tools to government - apps that are powerful enough for professionals, but simple and easy to use. During my time in this role, I was also deemed the teams Configuration Manager so I gained experience with the configuration and management of the project’s deployment pipelines using tools like Jenkins.

Outside of my work commitments I like hanging with my dog, playing video games or traveling. Other projects I’m working on include a text-adventure RPG and obtaining a sub-6 minute mile time.
